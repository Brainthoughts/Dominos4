Objective:
    There are two players who compete to remove all of their pieces by placing them next to similar ones
    You can only place a piece that touches one or more of the same number but no others.
The game ends if:
    Neither player can place.
    A player places all their pieces.
Keybindings:
    Space: skip turn
    A and D: rotate piece or move in inventory
    E: open inventory
    R: brings up these rules
    Left Click: place piece (must be in a valid location) or chose piece in inventory
    Right Click or Escape: restart the game
    W and S: flip piece
